The National Center for Tumor Diseases (NCT) in Germany is one of the key clinical partners in the NEARDATA project, contributing a use case focused on computer-assisted surgery. In laparoscopic and robotic procedures, NCT collects high-resolution video streams from endoscopic cameras to support AI-assisted decision-making.
